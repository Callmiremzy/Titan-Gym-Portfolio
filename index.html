Trainers

At Step Up Your Fitness, our trainers aren’t just instructors —
they’re motivators, mentors, and movement experts. Each member
of our training team is certified, experienced, and deeply passionate
about helping you unlock your full potential. Whether your goal is to
lose weight, gain muscle, improve mobility, or simply feel healthier,
our coaches are here to guide you every step of the way.

Nour Ahmed

A New Start After 30

Nour Ahmed, 34, worked a desk job and struggled with weight gain and low energy. He felt constantly tired and had lost confidence in himself — until he decided to join Step Up Your Fitness. In his first month, he lost 5 kg and felt an energy shift he hadn’t experienced in years. After 6 months, he had shed 18 kg and built muscle for the first time in his life. Now, Ahmed trains regularly and has become an inspiration to his friends and coworkers.

Sara Ali

Confidence After Motherhood

Sara, a mother of two, struggled with post-pregnancy weight gain and felt disconnected from her body. She tried multiple diets with no long-term results — until she joined the ladies’ program at Step Up. With customized workouts, supportive coaching, and a focus on mobility and mindset, she began to regain her strength. In just under 4 months, she lost 12 kg and felt reborn.

Our Featured
Services

At Step Up Your Fitness, we offer more than just workouts — we
deliver a full fitness experience tailored to your goals. Our featured
services are designed to help you train smarter, recover faster, and
feel your absolute best

  • Gym

    At Step Up Your Fitness, our gym isn’t just a room with equipment — it’s a space built for transformation. We offer a fully equipped gym floor with the latest machines, free weights, and functional training tools designed to support all types of workouts — from muscle building and toning to endurance and fat burning.

  • Circling

    At Step Up Your Fitness, our Circuit Training sessions are designed to give you maximum results in minimal time. This dynamic, high-intensity workout takes you through a series of exercise stations with little to no rest in between — keeping your heart rate up and your muscles challenged.

  • Aerobics

    Get your heart pumping and your body moving with our high-energy Aerobics classes at Step Up Your Fitness! Perfect for anyone who wants to burn calories, improve coordination, and have fun while getting fit. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned fitness lover, our aerobics sessions are designed to be easy to follow, super effective, and incredibly motivating.

  • Weight Lifting

    At Step Up Your Fitness, our Weight Lifting program is designed to help you unleash your full strength potential — whether you're a beginner learning proper form or an experienced lifter chasing new personal records.

  • Meditation

    At Step Up Your Fitness, we believe true wellness comes from within. Our Meditation sessions are designed to help you reset, refocus, and reconnect — whether you're dealing with stress, seeking clarity, or simply needing a moment of peace.

  • Crossfit

    At Step Up Your Fitness, our CrossFit program is built for those who want more than just a workout — they want challenge, intensity, and real results. CrossFit is a high-intensity, functional training system that blends strength, cardio, agility, and endurance into one powerful workout.
